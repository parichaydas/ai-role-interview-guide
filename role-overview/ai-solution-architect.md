# AI Solution Architect Role Overview

## Role Description

An AI Solution Architect designs comprehensive AI solutions tailored to specific business problems, bridging the gap between business requirements and technical implementation.

## Key Responsibilities

- Understand business requirements and translate them to AI solutions
- Design end-to-end AI solution architectures
- Evaluate and recommend technologies and platforms
- Define data requirements and integration points
- Establish success metrics and ROI frameworks
- Guide implementation and deployment strategies

## Key Skills

- Business acumen and problem-solving
- AI/ML knowledge and frameworks
- System architecture and design
- Technology evaluation and selection
- Communication and stakeholder management
- Project management

## Interview Focus Areas

- Business problem analysis
- Solution design and trade-offs
- Technology selection and evaluation
- Data strategy and architecture
- Implementation planning
- Cost-benefit analysis
- Scalability and future-proofing
