# AI Research Engineer Role Overview

## Role Description

An AI Research Engineer combines software engineering with research, working on cutting-edge AI technologies and pushing the boundaries of AI capabilities.

## Key Responsibilities

- Implement research papers and algorithms
- Conduct AI/ML experiments
- Optimize novel algorithms
- Contribute to research publications
- Bridge research and production

## Key Skills

- Deep understanding of ML/AI theory
- Research paper implementation
- Experimental design
- Advanced programming
- Mathematics and statistics

## Interview Focus Areas

- Deep learning fundamentals
- Current research topics
- Research paper implementation
- Experimental design and evaluation
- Mathematical foundations
- Programming and optimization
- System design for AI research
